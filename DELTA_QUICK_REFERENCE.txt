❓ FAQ

  Q: Does it work per-client?
  A: YES - each clientRunId has independent throttle window
  
  Q: Does buffering add latency?
  A: NO - dropped text is accumulated in-memory, included in next delta
  
  Q: Will all text reach the client?
  A: YES - final message sent without throttle ensures completeness
  
  Q: Can it run out of memory?
  A: NO - buffer is small (<40KB per 10k tokens)
  
  Q: What if I disable throttle (0ms)?
  A: All chunks sent immediately, 2x network traffic, snappier feel
  
  Q: How do I monitor it?
  A: Watch WebSocket messages for "state: delta" events
  
  Q: Can I adjust per-client?
  A: YES - see Option C in implementation guide
